BAOSHEN GUO

Southeast University. Nanjing.

Ph.d. Southeast University

I am a 4th-year Ph.D. student at Southeast University, under supervision of Prof. Shuai Wang. I am also working with Prof. Desheng Zhang and Prof. Tian He in research. Previously, I was a Graduate Research Trainee at the Cyber Physical Systems Lab, McGill University, under the supervision of Prof. Xue Liu.

My research interests are broadly in the areas of Cyber-Physical Systems, Human-Centered Computing, and Learning-based Decision-Making Applications in Smart Logistics.
You can contact me at guobaoshen AT seu DOT edu DOT cn. My publications can also be found at Google Scholar.

My long-term research goal is to build Robust, Interpretable, Safe, and Efficient decision-making technologies and AI model for real-world systems.

I will join M3S, part of MIT’ SMART program as a Postdoc in Fall 2024! I am open to research discussion and collaboration, please drop me an email if you are interested!
